\# Topic: The Psychology of B2B Email Subject Lines



Core Breakdown:

People don't buy what you sell; they buy how it makes them feel or how it solves a high-friction problem. When writing a B2B newsletter, your subject line must trigger a "curiosity gap" or "FOMO" (Fear Of Missing Out).



Tactical Framework Shared:

1\. The Anti-Subject Line: Short, lowercase, conversational (e.g., "quick question about Tuesday").

2\. The Data Hook: Put a striking stat in brackets first (e.g., "\[Case Study] 41% more pipeline with this email tweak").

3\. Focus on the Pain: Don't sell the feature; highlight the annoying problem the reader woke up with today.
